# Vela

**Vela** is a real-time infrastructure monitoring platform built for developer teams. Catch issues
before your users do — monitor, detect, and resolve incidents with full visibility into your stack.

## Why Vela?

Modern infrastructure is complex. Incidents happen, and every second of downtime costs teams time,
money, and trust. Vela gives engineering teams the observability layer they need to stay ahead of
outages — without the complexity of legacy monitoring tools.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Continuous visibility into your infrastructure health, with instant
  alerts when something goes wrong.
- **Instant Detection**: Identify anomalies and failures the moment they occur, before they escalate
  into incidents.
- **Fast Resolution**: Actionable insights that help teams diagnose and fix issues quickly, reducing
  mean time to recovery.
- **Seamless Integrations**: Connect with your existing stack in minutes. Works out of the box with
  the tools your team already uses.
- **Simple Pricing**: Transparent tiers — Free, Pro, and Enterprise — with no hidden fees and no
  lock-in.
